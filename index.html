Privacy Policy for Thirteen Bible Reading App

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Thirteen ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

CAMERA PERMISSION

  • Purpose: We request camera access to allow you to take profile pictures within the app
  • Usage: Photos are only used for your profile avatar and are stored locally on your device
  • Sharing: Profile pictures may be shared with other members of your Bible reading groups
  • Control: You can deny camera access and still use all other app features

PHOTO LIBRARY PERMISSION

  • Purpose: We request photo library access to allow you to select existing photos for your profile picture
  • Usage: We only access photos you specifically select
  • Sharing: Selected photos may be shared with other members of your Bible reading groups
  • Control: You can deny photo access and still use all other app features

DATA COLLECTION

  • We do not collect personal data beyond what you voluntarily provide
  • Your name and profile picture are shared only within your chosen Bible reading groups
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
